Hello and Welcome to cosy world of short stories!

  1. To create a story, please, register your profile. It would be nice if you write to your profile your Name(nickname), First name, Last name, email, city and country where you live.

  2. In your story can you add 3 sections, each of them contains a picture and a small text.

  3. Picture could be not less than the field for picture.

  4. After you have added the picture, you can crop it and save.

  5. The Story must have Title and Tags.

  6. Tags you can write through semicolon.

  7. Every section must have Headline and text minimum 150 and up to 300 characters

  8. To save a section of your story press right arrow in the bottom of the page.

  9. In your profile you can add/delete your profiles picture, go to edit your data and see all yours stories with possibility to edit/delete them.

Enjoy the opportunity to express yourself and share with the world yours travels, hobbies, emotions etc.
