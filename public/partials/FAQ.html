FAQ

Welcome to the world of short stories!

  1. To create a story, please register your user profile. You must include the following: username, first name, last name, e-mail, city and country where you live and a password.

  2. In your story you must add 3 sections, each of them contains a picture and text.

  3. Your picture must be the minimum size of the field on the screen..

  4. After you have chosen a picture, you can crop and save it.

  5. The Story must have a title and tags.

  6. Multiple tags can be added, they must be seperated by a comma.

  7. Every section must have headline and the story text should have a minimum of 150 and up to 300 characters.

  8. To save a section of your story press the right arrow at the bottom of the page.

  9. On your profile page you can add/delete your profile picture, edit your profile information and see all of your stories with the possibility to edit/delete them.

Enjoy the opportunity to express yourself and share with the world your travels, hobbies, emotions etc.
